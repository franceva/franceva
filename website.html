About Me:

Welcome to Peter Peabody Driving!

I’m Peter, a friendly and experienced driving instructor based in Basingstoke, serving learners across the surrounding areas. After spending years working with a leading national driving school, I decided to go freelance. This allows me to offer a more personal and flexible approach to help people achieve their driving goals. I specialize in creating a relaxed and supportive learning environment, making driving lessons enjoyable and stress-free for learners of all ages. Whether you’re a teenager just starting out or an adult returning to driving, I tailor each lesson to meet your needs and help you build confidence behind the wheel.

Facilities:

Modern Dual-Control Car: For maximum safety and comfort during lessons. Flexible Scheduling: Lessons designed to fit around your lifestyle. Expert Guidance: Practical tips and strategies for both the theory and practical tests. I’m dedicated to helping you succeed—whether that’s passing your driving test, gaining valuable road experience, or simply building your confidence on the road. With a proven track record and a passion for teaching, I’ll make sure you’re in safe hands.
